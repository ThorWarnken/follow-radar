Questions

Things people ask.

Is this safe?

Yes. The bookmarklet is JavaScript that runs in your own browser, on your own logged-in Instagram session, the same way the Instagram website does. It doesn't send your data anywhere except back to Flock, in your URL bar, where you can see it.

Will Instagram ban me for using this?

We don't think so, but we can't promise. The bookmarklet makes the same requests Instagram's own web app makes, and it deliberately goes slowly to look like a real person browsing. If Instagram throttles you, you might be temporarily blocked from viewing follower lists for half an hour. We don't know of anyone whose account has been banned for using a tool like this, but tools like this exist in a gray area.

It stopped halfway through. What happened?

Instagram throttled the requests. This is normal on big accounts. Wait about 30 minutes, then click the bookmarklet again on Instagram -- it remembers where it left off and picks up from there. We saved your progress.

Why is there a 10,000 follower cap?

Above 10k, the scan takes long enough that Instagram is much more likely to throttle you. If you really need it for a bigger account, the code is open source -- fork it and remove the cap.

Does this work on private accounts?

Yes, for your own account and any private account you already follow. The bookmarklet uses your own logged-in session, so it sees exactly what you see when you're scrolling Instagram normally.

What about the official Instagram data export?

That still works and is more reliable, but it's slow (Instagram emails it to you, can take a day) and the file format keeps changing. The bookmarklet is the fast version.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

What we collect

Nothing. Flock runs entirely in your browser. Your Instagram data (followers, following lists) is processed locally on your device and is never sent to any server.

How the bookmarklet works

The bookmarklet runs JavaScript in your browser on instagram.com, using your own logged-in session. It reads your followers and following lists from Instagram's web API -- the same endpoints Instagram's own website uses. The data is compressed and passed back to this site via the URL hash, which never leaves your browser.

Data storage

Your scan results may be temporarily stored in your browser's localStorage to preserve them during the payment flow. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser data.

Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe. When you click a pricing option, you are redirected to Stripe's hosted checkout page. We never see, handle, or store your payment information (credit card numbers, billing address, etc.). Stripe's privacy policy governs how they handle your payment data.

Cookies and tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking pixels. We do not run Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any similar service. We load fonts from Google Fonts and Fontshare (see third-party services below).

Third-party services

  • Google Fonts -- loads the Inter typeface. Subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • Fontshare -- loads the Satoshi typeface. Subject to Fontshare's terms.
  • Stripe -- processes payments. Subject to Stripe's privacy policy.
  • GitHub Pages -- hosts this website. Subject to GitHub's privacy statement.

Children

Flock is not intended for use by anyone under 13 years of age.
